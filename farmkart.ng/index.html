About us

Fresh From Our Farm, With Love

  • Who We Are
  • Our Vision
  • Our Mission

Farmkart Foods is an agri-tech company leveraging technology to grow sustainable products and by-products of poultry and fish. We deliver it from the farm to our customer's doorstep efficiently.

Our vision is to build a 360 degree technology-driven livestock farm and inspire the adaptation of tech livestock farming in Nigeria. 

Our mission is to optimise the entire value chain to make fish and poultry production sustainable, more accessible and affordable to Africans. A place where people can find livestock produce nutured specially for them.
